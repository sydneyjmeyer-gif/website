About

Sydney Meyer (she/her) is a writer, reporter, and self-described curious person born and raised in Louisville, Kentucky. She loves all things related to home, including horse racing, college basketball, and a generous glass of bourbon.

Sydney received her bachelor's degree from Boston University in May 2022. After graduating, she worked in pre-production for various Broadway shows before joining AmeriCorps, where she built affordable housing in rural Colorado.

She is currently working towards her Master of Science in Journalism at Northwestern University.
